Cookies & consent

This page explains how MO'CYNO uses cookies and similar technologies on mocyno.com, and how you can manage your choices.

1. What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit a website. It allows the site to recognise your browser and remember certain information for a limited period of time.

2. Cookies we use

MO'CYNO uses a limited number of cookies, grouped into the categories below.

2.1. Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function correctly and for your choices to be remembered (for example, your cookies consent). They cannot be disabled from our systems. You can still block them in your browser, but some parts of the site may not work properly.

2.2. Analytics / audience measurement cookies (Google Analytics 4)

With your consent, we use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to measure the audience and improve the performance of our website (most visited pages, error pages, browsing paths, etc.). The data collected is anonymised as much as possible and is used for statistical purposes only.

These cookies are only placed if you accept analytics cookies via the consent banner displayed on your first visit.

3. Consent and management of your choices

When you first visit the site, a banner informs you about the use of cookies and invites you to:

  • accept all cookies,
  • refuse non-essential cookies,
  • or customise your choices.

Your choice is stored for a limited period of time so that the banner does not appear on every visit.

Change your choice at any time

You can modify your consent at any time by reopening the cookies settings from this page.

4. Cookies lifetime

The lifetime of cookies used on this site does not exceed the legal limits in force. In particular, audience measurement cookies are kept for a maximum of 13 months, and consent choices are kept for a maximum of 6 months.

5. Managing cookies via your browser

Most browsers allow you to manage cookies (block them, delete them, accept them site by site, etc.). The configuration methods are specific to each browser:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Edge

For more information, see your browser’s help section.

6. Contact

For any question about this cookies policy, you can contact us at: contact@mocyno.com.
