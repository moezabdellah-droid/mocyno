Marina & Yacht Security

The marinas of the French Riviera are high-value zones requiring constant vigilance. MO'CYNO deploys security solutions tailored to port environments to protect yacht owners, crews and vessels against theft, vandalism and intrusion.

Our Port Security Services

  • Pontoon Access Control: Screening of entries, badge verification and delivery management.
  • Deterrence Patrols: Regular day and night patrols on the quays.
  • Vessel Surveillance: Visual inspection of moorings and on-board intrusion prevention.
  • Fire Safety: Trained officers (SSIAP) ready to intervene rapidly in case of fire or technical incidents.

Areas We Cover

We operate in the main ports of the PACA region, including Saint-Tropez, Cannes, Antibes, Monaco and Toulon. Our teams are accustomed to international clientele and the specific demands of the yachting industry.

FAQ – Marina Security

Do you offer 24/7 yacht surveillance?

Yes, we can dedicate a security officer exclusively to the surveillance of a vessel, whether docked or during private events on board.

Do your agents speak English?

Absolutely. Our agents operating in port areas are fluent in English to communicate effectively with international crews and owners.

Can you handle alarms on a boat?

Yes, as part of our patrols or dedicated surveillance, we perform immediate verification and alert authorities or the captain if necessary.
