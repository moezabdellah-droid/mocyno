FAQ – Hotel Security

Yes, English is a prerequisite for our hospitality agents. Some also speak Russian or Arabic depending on your needs.

Our agents wear the MO'CYNO suit and tie or can adopt your establishment's uniform.

Do you provide fire safety?

Yes, we provide team leaders and SSIAP 1/2/3 certified agents who are up to date with their training.
