Hotel & Hospitality Security

Hotels on the French Riviera welcome an international clientele demanding peace of mind and impeccable service. MO'CYNO integrates security as a component of the guest experience: our night auditors and "welcome security" agents ensure safety without disturbing comfort.

Services for Hotels

  • Qualified Night Auditor: Floor patrols, door locking and late check-in management.
  • Fire Safety (SSIAP): Mandatory for public buildings with sleeping quarters, our agents are trained in first aid and evacuation.
  • Discreet Screening: Access control to the lobby, parking and spa areas.
  • Conflict Management: Diplomatic intervention in case of noise or incivility.

Areas of Intervention

We work with boutique hotels, tourist residences and palaces in Nice, Cannes, Antibes and the Var hinterland.

FAQ – Hotel Security

Do your agents speak other languages?

Yes, English is a prerequisite for our hospitality agents. Some also speak Russian or Arabic depending on your needs.

How do you handle dress codes?

Our agents wear the MO'CYNO suit and tie or can adopt your establishment's uniform.

Do you provide fire safety?

Yes, we provide team leaders and SSIAP 1/2/3 certified agents who are up to date with their training.
