K9 Security Units — Dog-Handler Teams

Our K9 security units combine a trained dog with a certified handler to deliver a high level of deterrence and rapid threat detection. This duo is ideal for sensitive sites, isolated areas, large outdoor zones, construction sites and events requiring preventive surveillance.

The dog detects movements, intrusions and suspicious behaviour faster than any human sensor. The handler performs patrols, perimeter checks, access control and alarm response while applying strict legal and operational guidelines defined for K9 work in France.

MO'CYNO deploys K9 teams across the French Riviera, including Toulon, Saint-Tropez, Cannes and Nice. K9 intervention significantly increases deterrence and is highly effective when combined with manned guarding. Contact us for a tailored K9 plan.
