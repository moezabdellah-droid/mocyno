Manned Guarding — Security Officers

Manned guarding is the core of our business. MO'CYNO security officers provide a visible and deterrent presence on your sites: entrances, parking lots, sensitive zones, guard posts, reception halls, construction sites or residences. Their mission: prevent malicious acts, secure access points and react quickly in case of an incident.

MO'CYNO security agents ensure a reassuring presence upon request. Trained in conflict management and access control, they protect your premises, retail stores or private events.

Depending on your environment, we implement security patrols, alarm verification, badge controls, and inspections of zones or vehicles. Our agents follow precise instructions and maintain a digital logbook that traces important events: anomalies, interventions, calls, key handovers, etc.

Based in Toulon, MO'CYNO operates in PACA (Toulon, Saint-Tropez, Cannes, Nice...) for professional guarding missions, day and night. We adapt the profile of agents to the type of site (industrial, logistics, retail, residential, event) to reconcile welcome, security and image. Request a manned guarding quote.
