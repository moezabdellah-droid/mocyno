Manned Guarding — Security Officers

MO'CYNO provides professional manned guarding services across the French Riviera. Our trained and licensed security officers ensure a visible and effective presence on your sites: entrances, reception areas, construction sites, logistics platforms, car parks, residential buildings and commercial facilities. Their mission is to deter risks, protect people and assets, and respond quickly in case of incidents.

Depending on your needs, our officers perform security patrols, access control, badge checks, monitoring of sensitive areas, and alarm response (verification and reporting). Each officer follows clear operational procedures and provides documented reporting through a daily log or digital main courante.

Based in Toulon, we operate throughout the PACA region: Saint-Tropez, Cannes, Nice, Antibes and beyond. Whether you require short-term guarding, long-term site protection or reinforced night security, our team ensures a reliable, discreet and professional service. Request your security quote.
