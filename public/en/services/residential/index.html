Residential & Villa Security

Protecting your home, whether it is a primary or secondary residence, is an absolute priority. MO'CYNO offers guarding solutions adapted to isolated villas and prestigious condominiums, ensuring your peace of mind whether you are present or absent.

Our Residential Solutions

  • Static Guarding: Presence of a security guard 24/7 or at night.
  • Neighborhood Patrols: Random mobile patrols around your property.
  • Alarm Management: Immediate intervention (visual verification) in case of triggering.
  • Vacation Security: Reinforced surveillance during your holidays or prolonged absences.

Prime Locations

We ensure the surveillance of properties in Saint-Tropez, Mougins, Cap d'Antibes and the heights of Nice. Discretion is our watchword to preserve your privacy.

FAQ – Villa Security

Do you offer key holding services?

Yes, we can keep your keys in a secure safe to allow rapid intervention by our agents or access for emergency services.

Can I have a dog handler for my villa?

Yes, for large properties with grounds, a K9 unit is the most effective deterrent against intruders.

What is the minimum contract duration?

We are flexible: from one-off missions (evening, weekend) to annual contracts.
