Event Security

MO'CYNO provides comprehensive event security services for professional and private events: parties, brand launches, trade shows, concerts, weddings, festivals, sporting events, and more. Our teams ensure public safety, protect access points, and manage visitor flows effectively.

Depending on current regulations, our agents can perform screening, security pat-downs, visual bag checks, visitor guidance, and surveillance of sensitive areas (backstage, VIP zones, control rooms, stock). An operations manager coordinates the deployed teams and remains in contact with the organizer and, if necessary, public authorities.

Based in Toulon, we operate throughout the French Riviera (Saint-Tropez, Cannes, Nice, Monaco) for premium events where image and hospitality are as important as safety. We adapt our agents' attire, behavior and numbers to your specific requirements. Discuss your event with us for a tailored quote.
