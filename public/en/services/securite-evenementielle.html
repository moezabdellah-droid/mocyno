Event Security

MO'CYNO provides comprehensive event security services for professional and private events: corporate functions, luxury brand activations, weddings, concerts, festivals and VIP receptions. Our teams ensure a safe, well-organised environment while maintaining a smooth visitor experience.

Depending on legal requirements, our officers manage access control, screening, bag checks, ticket verification, flow management and surveillance of sensitive zones (backstage, VIP lounges, control rooms, storage areas). A dedicated event supervisor coordinates all security personnel and liaises with event organisers and external authorities when needed.

Based in Toulon, we operate throughout the French Riviera — Saint-Tropez, Cannes, Nice, Monaco — and offer tailor-made, discreet and professional solutions. Discuss your event with us.
