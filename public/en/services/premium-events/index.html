Premium Event Security

Organizing prestigious events on the French Riviera requires flawless security. MO'CYNO provides high-end solutions for galas, festivals, VIP weddings and corporate events. We combine absolute discretion with operational efficiency.

Event Expertise

  • VIP Welcome & Screening: Guest list management with courtesy and firmness.
  • Close Protection: CPO officers for VIPs and celebrities.
  • Asset Protection: Surveillance of technical installations, control rooms and gifts.
  • Security Chauffeurs: Secure transport of guests from airports or hotels.

Local References

We regularly secure private events in villas in Saint-Tropez and Saint-Jean-Cap-Ferrat, as well as public gatherings during the Cannes Film Festival or the Monaco Grand Prix.

FAQ – Event Security

What do your agents wear?

For premium events, our agents wear dark suits and earpieces to seamlessly blend into your atmosphere.

Do you handle coordination with the police?

Yes, for large events, our operations manager liaises with the relevant public authorities.

Can you secure a private beach?

Absolutely, we protect both maritime and land access to private beaches during summer parties.
