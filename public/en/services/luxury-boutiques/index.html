Luxury Retail & Boutique Security

Luxury fashion houses and high-end boutiques face specific risks: high-value theft, robberies and managing a demanding clientele. MO'CYNO trains its agents in luxury codes: impeccable presentation, customer service skills and discreet vigilance.

Our Luxury Retail Services

  • Loss Prevention: Active surveillance of products and suspicious behavior (shrinkage reduction).
  • Access Control: Managing customer entry (buzzer, airlock) and VIP welcome.
  • Secure Opening & Closing: Escorting staff during fund or jewelry movements.
  • Static Guarding: "Doorman" style agents providing both prestige and deterrence.

Where We Operate

We support major brands in fashion, jewelry and watchmaking in Cannes (La Croisette), Monaco (Carré d'Or) and Saint-Tropez.

FAQ – Retail Security

How do you select agents for luxury retail?

We recruit based on strict criteria: excellent oral communication, charisma, emotional stability and prior experience in high-end service.

Can the agent wear our brand's suit?

Yes, whilst respecting regulations (visible insignia), our agents can wear a suit provided by the brand for total visual harmony.

Do you cover pop-up stores?

Yes, we secure private sales and temporary showrooms, often organized in hotels or private villas.
