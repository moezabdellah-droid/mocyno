Construction Site Security

Construction and renovation sites are particularly vulnerable to theft of materials (copper, tools, fuel) and vandalism. MO'CYNO ensures rigorous surveillance to guarantee the continuity of your works and avoid costly delays.

Solutions for Construction Sites

  • Night & Weekend Guarding: Human presence during closing hours to deter intrusion.
  • K9 Security: Use of dog handlers for maximum efficiency on large perimeters.
  • Site Access Control: Verification of subcontractors, badges and deliveries.
  • Digital Reporting: Full traceability of patrols and incidents.

Local Expertise

We secure construction sites throughout the Var and Alpes-Maritimes, ranging from luxury villa renovations to real estate developments and public infrastructure.

FAQ – Site Security

What is the best solution for a large open site?

For large open areas, we recommend a K9 unit (dog handler), whose detection and deterrence capabilities are superior to those of a lone guard.

Do you operate on short-term sites?

Yes, we offer flexible contracts for specific needs (delivery of sensitive materials, bank holiday weekends, etc.).

Do you have the necessary equipment (PPE)?

Absolutely. Our agents are equipped with full PPE (safety shoes, helmets, high-vis vests) and respect site safety regulations.
