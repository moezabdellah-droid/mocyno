Fire Safety — SSIAP Officers

MO'CYNO provides SSIAP-certified fire safety officers (1/2/3) for ERP and industrial sites across the French Riviera. Our fire wardens are trained to monitor safety systems, ensure regulatory compliance and implement emergency procedures in case of fire or technical incidents.

Officers stationed at the control room or patrolling the premises conduct regular checks: emergency exits, extinguishers, smoke vents, alarm systems and safety pathways. In the event of an alert, they manage the evacuation, perform the alarm verification, assist occupants and coordinate with emergency services.

Our SSIAP teams operate across the PACA region, including Toulon, Cannes, Nice and Saint-Tropez. We design fire safety setups tailored to your site classification, schedules and operational constraints. Request your SSIAP assessment.
