Fire Safety — SSIAP Officers

MO'CYNO provides SSIAP 1/2/3 teams to ensure fire safety in Public Access Buildings (ERP), industrial sites, parking lots, malls or office buildings. Our SSIAP agents ensure safety equipment works properly, prevent risks, and enforce fire safety regulations.

Stationed at the Security Control Center or on patrol, they perform regular checks: emergency exits, safety lighting, smoke extraction, extinguishers, technical alarms... In case of an alarm or fire outbreak, they perform doubt removal, trigger appropriate procedures, assist with public evacuation and coordinate with emergency services.

Our SSIAP teams operate across the PACA region, including Toulon, Saint-Tropez, Cannes and Nice. We design fire safety setups tailored to your site classification, schedules and operational constraints. To secure your premises sustainably and meet regulatory obligations, request a SSIAP study from MO'CYNO.
