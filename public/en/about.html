About MO'CYNO

MO'CYNO is a private security company based in the French Riviera, specialising in manned guarding, K9 dog-handler units, SSIAP fire safety, and event security. From Toulon, we support clients in Saint-Tropez, Cannes, Nice, and across the entire Côte d’Azur to secure sites, events, retail spaces, logistics areas and private residences.

Our approach is built on three pillars: operational excellence, transparency, and responsiveness. Every assignment begins with a risk assessment and an on-site review to define clear procedures, escalation paths, and the appropriate reporting method (daily log, incident reports, indicators). Our teams are supervised and monitored to ensure consistent and reliable service, including for long-term deployments.

MO'CYNO operates under an official CNAPS licence and complies with the French Internal Security Code. We select highly trained and committed security officers and K9 handlers capable of providing both robust protection and representing your brand image with professionalism.

Our Standards

  • Qualified and carefully selected officers with a strong service culture.
  • Clear procedures, supervision, and continuous operational feedback.
  • 24/7 responsiveness and transparent reporting.
