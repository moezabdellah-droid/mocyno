Frequently asked questions

Which types of sites does MO'CYNO protect?

We secure industrial and logistics facilities, shops, car parks, residential buildings and construction sites, as well as public and private events in the PACA region.

Do you provide fast quotes?

Yes. Once we have reviewed your needs, we usually send a tailored quote within 24 business hours.

Are your K9 handlers trained and declared?

All our K9 handlers have the required certifications, operational experience and are declared in accordance with the French Private Security Code.

Is MO'CYNO authorised by the CNAPS?

Yes. MO'CYNO operates under a CNAPS licence, displayed in the footer of the site, in line with legal requirements. This licence does not confer any public authority status or official endorsement.

Do you only work in Toulon?

No. We are based in Toulon but operate throughout the PACA region: Hyères, La Seyne, Saint-Tropez, Sainte-Maxime, Fréjus, Saint-Raphaël, Cannes, Antibes, Nice, and more.

Can you secure a one-off event?

Yes. We design a tailored security setup for your event (welcome, access control, screening, patrols, first aid support) taking into account your venue, schedule and expected audience.

What is the emergency response time for an alarm in the Var?

Our 24/7 security control room triggers immediate intervention by a mobile patrol or K9 unit upon alarm. In Toulon, Hyères and the Gulf of Saint-Tropez, we guarantee rapid arrival to secure the premises.

Do you ensure confidentiality for luxury villa security?

Yes. Discretion is absolute. Our close protection and guarding agents are trained in luxury standards and sign a strict confidentiality charter to protect the privacy of your residences and VIP events.

Do you offer geolocated patrol reports?

Yes. We use electronic patrol monitoring technology (PTI/DATI). You receive real-time timestamped and geolocated intervention reports, ensuring total transparency of our security services.

Is the security quote free and non-binding?

Absolutely. Request your free quote for manned guarding, K9 or fire safety. We perform a complimentary security audit if necessary to precisely assess your needs in the Var.

We usually answer within 24 business hours.
