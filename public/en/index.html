Frequently Asked Questions

Which types of sites does MO'CYNO protect?

We secure industrial and logistics facilities, shops, car parks, residential buildings and construction sites, as well as public and private events in the PACA region.

Do you provide fast quotes?

Yes. Once we have reviewed your needs, we send a tailored quote within 24 business hours.

Are your K9 handlers trained and declared?

All our K9 handlers have the required certifications, operational experience and are declared in accordance with the French Private Security Code.

Is MO'CYNO authorised by the CNAPS?

Yes. MO'CYNO operates under CNAPS licence AUT-83-2124-09-09-20250998415. This licence does not confer any public authority status or official endorsement.

Do you only work in Toulon?

No. We are based in Toulon but operate throughout the PACA region: Saint-Tropez, Sainte-Maxime, Fréjus, Cannes, Antibes, Nice, and more.

Can you secure a one-off event?

Yes. We design a tailored security plan (access control, screening, patrols, first aid) based on your venue, schedule and audience.

Is the security quote free and non-binding?

Absolutely. Request your free security assessment — no commitment. We conduct a complimentary on-site audit if needed.
