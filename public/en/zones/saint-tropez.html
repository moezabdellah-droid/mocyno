Why choose a K9 unit in Saint-Tropez?

The geographical configuration of Saint-Tropez and the peninsula, with its vast wooded properties and multiple access points, makes classic human monitoring sometimes insufficient. The K9 officer brings critical added value:

  • Sharpened senses of the dog: Capable of detecting a presence hidden in the vegetation well before the human eye or a camera.
  • Psychological deterrence: The presence of a trained defense dog immediately discourages almost all intrusion attempts.
  • Intervention speed: On a large estate, the dog can neutralize or block an escape very quickly.
