Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to common questions about our guarding services, K9 units, and security operations on the French Riviera.

Do you operate in Saint-Tropez, Cannes and Monaco?

Yes, MO'CYNO deploys K9 units and security agents for villas, events and sensitive sites across the entire French Riviera, including Saint-Tropez, Cannes, Nice and Monaco.

Are your K9 handlers fully licensed?

Absolutely. All our handlers and security officers are certified, trained, and registered with the French CNAPS authority, ensuring fully compliant and professional service.

What is your emergency response time for an alarm?

Our 24/7 security center triggers immediate intervention. In the Saint-Tropez or Cannes areas, we aim for a rapid arrival to secure your property.

Do you provide residential security for luxury villas?

Yes, we specialize in high-end villa protection, combining discretion with effectiveness. Our agents are trained for luxury environments and VIP privacy.

Is the security quote free of charge?

Yes. Any quote for guarding, K9 or event security is free and carries no obligation. We can also provide a security audit of your site if needed.

Can you provide multiple agents for large events?

Yes, we can deploy full teams of security officers, K9 handlers and fire safety agents for large-scale events, festivals and private parties.
